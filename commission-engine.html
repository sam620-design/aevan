Smart Commission System

Automating complex financial distributions with precision.

The Problem

Manual commission calculation was the single biggest bottleneck in the client's operations. Managing multi-tier slab structures, EMI-based release cycles, and advisor upline distributions meant that errors were frequent, and payouts took weeks to process.

The Solution

We engineered a custom "Commission Engine" that handles complex logic automatically. The system tracks every sale, identifies the correct slab based on volume, and schedules payouts based on customer EMI payments, ensuring financial integrity at every step.

Key Features

Slab Engine

Dynamic commission percentage calculation based on sales volume milestones.

EMI Release Logic

Automated commission release triggered by customer payment milestones.

Upline Distribution

Smart lead-based distribution to managers and referral partners.

Transparent Payouts

Advisors can track their earnings and upcoming payouts in real-time.

The Result

Financial processing time was reduced from 14 days to just 24 hours. The elimination of manual errors restored trust among advisors, leading to a 30% increase in active sales agents within the first quarter.
