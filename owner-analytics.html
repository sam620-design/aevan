Owner Analytics Dashboard

Centralizing business intelligence for real-time decision making.

The Problem

Before this system, the business owners had no centralized way to view their performance. Sales data, commission logs, and advisor payouts were scattered across multiple spreadsheets and manual ledgers, leading to delays in insights and potential errors in financial forecasting.

The Solution

We built a custom, read-only analytics dashboard that aggregates data from various sources into a single, high-fidelity interface. This provided the owners with immediate visibility into sales trends, revenue distribution, and advisor performance metrics without the need for manual intervention.

Key Features

Real-time Insights

Live tracking of sales and revenue as they happen in the field.

Advisor Performance

Deep-dive analytics into individual advisor conversion rates and volume.

Payment Tracking

Comprehensive visibility into EMI schedules and outstanding payouts.

Audit Logs

Every data point is tracked for transparency and future auditing.

The Result

The dashboard transformed the business from a reactive state to a data-driven powerhouse. Management now makes strategic decisions in minutes rather than days, with 100% confidence in the data accuracy.
